Frequently Asked Questions

What are your operating hours?

Our burger shop is open from 7 AM to 7 PM every day of the week. You can visit us during these hours to enjoy our delicious burgers and other menu items.

Do you offer vegetarian or vegan options?

Yes, we have vegetarian and vegan options available on our menu. We understand the importance of catering to different dietary preferences and strive to provide a variety of choices for all our customers.

Can I place an order for delivery?

Currently, we offer deli service and pick-up service, but we do not provide direct delivery. You can place an order for pick-up and collect it at our shop. We ensure that your order is carefully prepared and packaged for your convenience.

Can I make special requests or customize my order?

Absolutely! We understand that everyone has unique preferences and dietary needs. You can make special requests or customize your order based on your preferences. Our friendly staff will be happy to assist you and accommodate your requests to the best of our ability.

Do you offer catering services for events?

Yes, we provide catering services for events. Whether it's a corporate gathering, birthday party, or any other special occasion, our team can work with you to create a customized catering menu that suits your requirements. Please contact us in advance to discuss your event and catering needs.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept cash as well as major credit and debit cards for payment. We strive to provide convenient payment options to ensure a seamless experience for our customers.

Is there parking available?

We have parking spaces available for our customers. You can conveniently park your vehicle near our burger shop while you enjoy your meal.

Can I make modifications to the ingredients in a burger?

Absolutely! We understand that personal taste preferences vary. You can customize your burger by requesting modifications to the ingredients. Whether you want to add extra toppings, remove certain ingredients, or adjust the sauce, our team will do their best to accommodate your preferences.

Are your meat patties made from fresh ingredients?

Yes, our meat patties are made from high-quality, fresh ingredients. We take pride in using premium cuts of meat and carefully crafting our patties to ensure a delicious and satisfying burger experience. We prioritize the freshness and flavor of our ingredients to deliver the best taste to our customers.

Are there options for customers with food allergies?

We understand the importance of catering to customers with food allergies. While we make efforts to prevent cross-contamination, it's important to note that our kitchen handles various ingredients, including common allergens. Please inform our staff about your specific allergies, and we will do our best to accommodate your needs and provide you with suitable options.
